A father is the pillar of the family. He symbolizes dedication, sacrifice, and hard work. Fathers face countless challenges to ensure their children's happiness. They work tirelessly day and night so that every member of the family can live comfortably and have their needs met. Often, a father's affection is hidden behind his strictness, but deep down, he has immense love for his children. Fathers work hard to guide their children in the right direction and to help them become self-reliant. A father's love is invaluable, providing us with protection and guidance throughout our lives.
